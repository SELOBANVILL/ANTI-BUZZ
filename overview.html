Overview

01

Purpose

Immoderate and uncontrolled noise disrupts students' ability to study and prevents certain areas from serving as effective spaces for learning. Anti-Buzz automatically detects excessive noise and rings a bell to help monitor and control noise in environments where silence and order are important.

02

How It Works

A microphone continuously detects sound levels. The Arduino Uno processes the input and compares it to a predefined threshold.

🔇 Below Threshold — System continues monitoring
🔔 Exceeds Threshold — Servo motor activates, bell rings for a set duration
This loop enables real-time noise monitoring and automatic response.
03

Benefits

Automated noise management
No human supervision needed
Cost-effective & adaptable
